## Getting Started

### What is Family Hub?

Family Hub is a comprehensive platform designed to help families stay connected and organized. It brings together messaging, photo sharing, calendar management, shopping lists, and video calling in one secure, easy-to-use application.

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the app** at `https://familyhub.app`
2. **Click "Sign Up"**
3. **Enter your details:**
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Create a secure password (8+ characters, mix of letters and numbers)
4. **Verify your email** - Check your inbox for a verification link
5. **Complete your profile** - Add a profile photo and bio (optional)

### Logging In

1. **Go to the login page**
2. **Enter your email and password**
3. **Click "Sign In"**
4. **Two-factor authentication** (if enabled) - Enter the code from your authenticator app

### First Time Setup

After logging in for the first time:

1. **Create or join a family** (see [Family Setup](#family-setup))
2. **Invite family members** to join
3. **Customize your preferences** in Settings
4. **Explore the features** - Start with messaging or the photo gallery

## Account Management

### Updating Your Profile

1. **Click your profile icon** (top right)
2. **Select "Profile Settings"**
3. **Edit your information:**
   - Name
   - Email
   - Profile photo
   - Bio/About
4. **Click "Save Changes"**

### Changing Your Password

1. **Go to Settings** → **Security**
2. **Click "Change Password"**
3. **Enter your current password**
4. **Enter your new password** (8+ characters)
5. **Confirm the new password**
6. **Click "Update Password"**

### Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account.

1. **Go to Settings** → **Security**
2. **Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. **Choose your method:**
   - **Authenticator App** (recommended) - Use Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy
   - **SMS** - Receive codes via text message
4. **Follow the setup instructions**
5. **Save backup codes** in a safe place
6. **Click "Enable"**

### Deactivating Your Account

⚠️ **Warning:** This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

1. **Go to Settings** → **Account**
2. **Scroll to "Danger Zone"**
3. **Click "Deactivate Account"**
4. **Confirm by typing your email address**
5. **Click "Permanently Delete"**

## Family Setup

### Creating a Family

1. **After logging in, click "Create Family"**
2. **Enter family details:**
   - Family name (e.g., "The Smiths")
   - Description (optional)
   - Family photo (optional)
3. **Click "Create"**
4. **You're now the family administrator**

### Joining an Existing Family

1. **Ask a family member for an invitation link**
2. **Click the link** or go to **"Join Family"** and enter the code
3. **Review the family details**
4. **Click "Join"**
5. **You're now a member of the family**

### Inviting Family Members

1. **Go to Family Settings** (click family name → Settings)
2. **Click "Invite Members"**
3. **Choose invitation method:**
   - **Email** - Enter email addresses (comma-separated)
   - **Invite Link** - Share a link with family members
   - **QR Code** - Scan with a phone camera
4. **Click "Send Invitations"**
5. **Family members will receive invitations** and can join

### Managing Family Members

**As a family administrator:**

1. **Go to Family Settings** → **Members**
2. **View all family members** and their roles
3. **Change member roles:**
   - **Administrator** - Full access to all features and settings
   - **Member** - Access to all features
   - **Guest** - Limited access (view-only for some features)
4. **Remove a member** - Click the member, then "Remove from Family"

## Messaging

### Sending a Message

1. **Click "Messages"** in the main menu
2. **Select a conversation** or click **"New Message"**
3. **Type your message** in the text box
4. **Add attachments** (optional) - Click the paperclip icon
5. **Press Enter** or click **"Send"**

### Creating a Group Chat

1. **Click "Messages"** → **"New Group"**
2. **Enter a group name** (e.g., "Planning Committee")
3. **Select members** to add to the group
4. **Click "Create Group"**
5. **Start chatting!**

### Message Features

- **Reactions** - Hover over a message and click the emoji icon to react
- **Replies** - Click the reply arrow to respond to a specific message
- **Editing** - Click the three dots on your message and select "Edit"
- **Deleting** - Click the three dots and select "Delete"
- **Pinning** - Pin important messages to the top of the conversation
- **Search** - Use the search box to find messages by keyword

### Notifications

**Control how you're notified:**

1. **Go to Settings** → **Notifications**
2. **Choose notification preferences:**
   - **All Messages** - Get notified for every message
   - **Mentions Only** - Only when someone mentions you
   - **Muted** - No notifications
3. **Set quiet hours** - No notifications between specific times
4. **Choose notification method:**
   - Push notifications (in-app)
   - Email notifications
   - SMS notifications

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Family Hub free?**
A: Yes! Family Hub is free to use with basic features. We also offer a Premium plan with additional storage and features.

**Q: How many family members can I invite?**
A: You can invite unlimited family members to your family.

**Q: Can I have multiple families?**
A: Yes, you can create or join multiple families. Switch between families using the family selector.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can download all your data at any time from Settings → Data & Storage.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, go to Settings → Profile and update your email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings → Account → Danger Zone and click "Deactivate Account". This action is permanent.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, you can create multiple accounts with different email addresses.

### Messaging Questions

**Q: Can I unsend a message?**
A: Yes, click the three dots on your message and select "Delete". The message will be removed for everyone.

**Q: Can I search for old messages?**
A: Yes, use the search box in the Messages section to find messages by keyword.

**Q: Can I mute a conversation?**
A: Yes, click the conversation settings and select "Mute". You won't receive notifications but can still see messages.

**Q: Can I delete a conversation?**
A: Yes, but the conversation will still exist for other members. You can archive it instead.

### Photo Gallery Questions

**Q: What file formats are supported?**
A: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and HEIC. Videos are also supported (MP4, MOV).

**Q: What's the maximum file size?**
A: Individual files up to 100 MB. Batch uploads up to 1 GB.

**Q: Can I edit photos in the app?**
A: Yes, click a photo and use the editing tools to crop, rotate, adjust brightness, and apply filters.

**Q: Can I download photos?**
A: Yes, if you have download permissions. Click a photo and select "Download".

### Calendar Questions

**Q: Can I sync with my personal calendar?**
A: Yes, go to Settings → Integrations and connect your Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar.

**Q: Can I set recurring events?**
A: Yes, when creating an event, select "Recurring" and choose the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly).

**Q: Can I get reminders for events?**
A: Yes, click an event and add reminders. You can set multiple reminders for the same event.

**Q: Can I see other family members' calendars?**
A: Yes, you can view all family members' calendars. You can also hide specific calendars.

### Shopping List Questions

**Q: Can I share a shopping list with specific people?**
A: Yes, click a list and select "Share". Choose which family members can see it.

**Q: Can I assign items to people?**
A: Yes, click an item and select "Assign To" to assign it to a family member.

**Q: Can I see prices for items?**
A: Yes, the app shows estimated prices based on local stores. Prices may vary.

**Q: Can I add recipes to my shopping list?**
A: Yes, find a recipe in the app or paste a recipe URL, and the ingredients will be added to your list.

### Video Call Questions

**Q: How many people can be on a call?**
A: Up to 100 people can be on a single call.

**Q: Can I record calls?**
A: Yes, but all participants must consent to recording. Recordings are stored securely.

**Q: Can I use a virtual background?**
A: Yes, go to call settings and choose a background image or blur your background.

**Q: What's the minimum internet speed needed?**
A: For video calls, we recommend at least 2.5 Mbps upload and download speed.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem:** I'm getting an error when trying to log in.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your email and password** - Make sure you're using the correct credentials
2. **Reset your password** - Click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions
3. **Check your internet connection** - Make sure you're connected to the internet
4. **Clear your browser cache** - Delete cookies and cached data
5. **Try a different browser** - Some browsers may have compatibility issues
6. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### Can't Send Messages

**Problem:** Messages aren't sending or I'm getting an error.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your internet connection** - Make sure you're connected
2. **Check message length** - Messages must be under 5,000 characters
3. **Refresh the page** - Press F5 or Ctrl+R
4. **Clear browser cache** - Delete cookies and cached data
5. **Try a different browser** - Some browsers may have issues
6. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### Photos Won't Upload

**Problem:** I'm trying to upload photos but they're not uploading.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check file size** - Individual files must be under 100 MB
2. **Check file format** - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, HEIC
3. **Check internet connection** - Make sure you have a stable connection
4. **Check storage quota** - You may have reached your storage limit
5. **Try uploading one photo** - Test with a single photo first
6. **Clear browser cache** - Delete cookies and cached data
7. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### Video Call Issues

**Problem:** I can't hear or see the other person on a video call.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check camera and microphone permissions** - Allow access in your browser settings
2. **Check internet connection** - Make sure you have a stable, fast connection
3. **Restart the call** - End the call and start a new one
4. **Check camera and microphone** - Make sure they're not being used by another app
5. **Update your browser** - Make sure you're using the latest version
6. **Try a different browser** - Some browsers may have compatibility issues
7. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### Calendar Events Not Syncing

**Problem:** Events aren't syncing with my external calendar.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check calendar connection** - Go to Settings → Integrations and verify the connection
2. **Re-authorize the connection** - Disconnect and reconnect your calendar
3. **Check calendar permissions** - Make sure you've granted the necessary permissions
4. **Wait for sync** - Syncing can take up to 15 minutes
5. **Refresh the page** - Press F5 or Ctrl+R
6. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### Notifications Not Working

**Problem:** I'm not receiving notifications.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check notification settings** - Go to Settings → Notifications and enable notifications
2. **Check browser permissions** - Allow notifications in your browser settings
3. **Check quiet hours** - Make sure quiet hours aren't active
4. **Check muted conversations** - Unmute conversations you want to be notified about
5. **Refresh the page** - Press F5 or Ctrl+R
6. **Clear browser cache** - Delete cookies and cached data
7. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### App Running Slowly

**Problem:** The app is slow or laggy.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check internet connection** - Make sure you have a fast, stable connection
2. **Close other tabs** - Close unnecessary browser tabs to free up memory
3. **Clear browser cache** - Delete cookies and cached data
4. **Update your browser** - Make sure you're using the latest version
5. **Restart your device** - Restart your computer or phone
6. **Try a different browser** - Some browsers may perform better
7. **Contact support** - If the problem persists, reach out to our support team

### Storage Full

**Problem:** I'm getting a storage full error.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check storage usage** - Go to Settings → Data & Storage to see what's using space
2. **Delete old photos** - Archive or delete photos you no longer need
3. **Delete old messages** - Archive or delete old conversations
4. **Upgrade to Premium** - Get unlimited storage with a Premium subscription
5. **Contact support** - If you need help freeing up space, reach out to our support team
