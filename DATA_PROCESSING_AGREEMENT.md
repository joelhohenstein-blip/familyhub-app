## 2. Definitions

### 2.1 Key Terms

**Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

**Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data (collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, erasure, etc.).

**Data Subject:** The individual to whom personal data relates.

**Data Controller:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.

**Data Processor:** The entity that processes personal data on behalf of the controller.

**Sub-processor:** A processor engaged by the processor to carry out specific processing activities.

**Processing Instructions:** Written instructions from the controller specifying what, how, and why the processor should process personal data.

**Data Breach:** Unauthorized or accidental destruction, loss, alteration, disclosure of, or access to personal data.

**GDPR:** General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679.

**Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs):** EU-approved contractual terms for international data transfers.

## 3. Scope and Applicability

### 3.1 When This DPA Applies

This DPA applies when:
- FamilyHub processes personal data on behalf of the customer
- The customer is the data controller
- Processing is subject to GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, or similar regulations
- The customer has requested a DPA

### 3.2 When This DPA Does NOT Apply

This DPA does NOT apply when:
- The customer is using FamilyHub's standard consumer services
- FamilyHub is the data controller (not the processor)
- Processing is not subject to data protection regulations
- The customer has not executed this DPA

### 3.3 Precedence

In case of conflict between this DPA and other agreements:
1. This DPA takes precedence for data protection matters
2. The Terms of Service applies to all other matters
3. The Privacy Policy describes FamilyHub's data practices

## 9. Data Breach Notification

### 9.1 Breach Definition

A data breach is unauthorized or accidental:
- Destruction of personal data
- Loss of personal data
- Alteration of personal data
- Disclosure of personal data
- Access to personal data

### 9.2 Notification Timeline

FamilyHub will notify the customer:

- **Immediately:** Upon discovery of a breach (within 24 hours)
- **Investigation:** Provide updates every 48 hours during investigation
- **Final Report:** Complete report within 72 hours
- **Authorities:** Notify authorities if required by law

### 9.3 Breach Notification Content

Notifications will include:

- **Description:** What data was affected
- **Scope:** How many data subjects affected
- **Timeline:** When the breach occurred and was discovered
- **Impact:** What risks the breach poses
- **Measures:** What steps are being taken
- **Contact:** Who to contact for more information
- **Recommendations:** What data subjects should do

### 9.4 Breach Investigation

FamilyHub will:

1. **Contain:** Stop the breach immediately
2. **Investigate:** Determine scope and impact
3. **Remediate:** Fix the vulnerability
4. **Document:** Record all details
5. **Report:** Provide findings to customer
6. **Prevent:** Implement measures to prevent recurrence
