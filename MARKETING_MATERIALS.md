### FAQ Answers

**Q: Is FamilyHub really completely free?**
A: Yes! FamilyHub is 100% free, forever. Unlimited family members, messaging, photos, video calls, calendar, shopping lists—everything. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no surprises. We're funded by donations and believe families shouldn't have to pay to stay connected.

**Q: How many family members can I add?**
A: Unlimited! Add your entire family—parents, kids, grandparents, cousins, aunts, uncles, everyone. Add as many as you want, and it never costs extra.

**Q: How much photo storage do I get?**
A: Unlimited! Upload as many photos and videos as you want. FamilyHub has no storage limits. Share your whole life together.

**Q: Is my family data private?**
A: 100% yes. Your family data is encrypted end-to-end, isolated to your family only, and we never share it with anyone. We never sell your data, never show ads, never track you. Privacy is built in.

**Q: What's the catch?**
A: No catch! We built FamilyHub for our own families first. It's free because we believe families should be able to stay connected without worrying about cost. If you love it and want to support us, you can donate a coffee ($5) or more—but it's completely optional.
