## 🍪 Cookie Disclosure Summary

**This is the most important legal requirement you asked about.**

### Cookies FamilyHub Uses

**Essential Cookies (Required for app to work):**
1. `__clerk_db_jwt` - Clerk authentication token
2. `__clerk_client_uat` - Clerk auth timestamp
3. `__session` - Session management

**Functional Cookies (Improve experience):**
4. `language` - Language preference (localStorage)
5. UI state cookies - Remember user preferences

**Third-Party Cookies (Required for features):**
6. `jitsi_session` - Video calls (Jitsi)
7. `pusher_session` - Real-time messaging (Pusher)
8. `stripe_session` - Payments (Stripe)
9. OAuth cookies - GitHub/Google login

**Analytics Cookies (Optional, can be disabled):**
- Sentry error tracking
- Vercel analytics

### User Control
- Users can disable non-essential cookies in browser settings
- Users can opt out of analytics
- Essential cookies cannot be disabled (app won't work)
- Clear instructions provided in Cookie Policy

### Legal Statement
The **COOKIE_POLICY.md** file provides:
- ✅ Clear disclosure of all cookies
- ✅ Explanation of why each cookie is used
- ✅ Instructions for disabling cookies
- ✅ User rights and control options
- ✅ Compliance with GDPR and CCPA
