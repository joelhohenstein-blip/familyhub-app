## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit FamilyHub**: Go to https://familyhub.app
2. **Click "Get Started Free"**
3. **Enter your email** and create a password
4. **Verify your email** (check your inbox for verification link)
5. **Complete your profile**:
   - Upload a profile photo
   - Set your display name
   - Choose your preferred language

### Your First Family

After creating your account, you'll be prompted to:

1. **Create a family** or **join an existing family**
2. **Set your family surname** (e.g., "Smith", "Johnson")
3. **Invite family members** via email
4. **Start using FamilyHub!**

## Family Management

### Creating a Family

1. Go to **Families** → **Create New Family**
2. Enter your **family surname**
3. Add a **family description** (optional)
4. Click **Create Family**

### Inviting Family Members

1. Go to **Family Settings** → **Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their **email address**
4. Select their **role**:
   - **Admin**: Full access, can manage members
   - **Member**: Can access all features
   - **Guest**: Limited access (view-only)
5. Click **Send Invite**

The invited member will receive an email with a link to join your family.

### Managing Members

1. Go to **Family Settings** → **Members**
2. View all family members and their roles
3. **Edit member**: Click the member's name to change their role
4. **Remove member**: Click the ⋮ menu → **Remove**

### Family Roles & Permissions

| Role | Create Posts | Create Events | Manage Members | Delete Content | View All |
|------|--------------|---------------|----------------|----------------|----------|
| Admin | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Member | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | Own only | ✅ |
| Guest | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

## Messaging & Communication

### Family Message Board

The **Message Board** is where your family shares updates, photos, and conversations.

#### Creating a Post

1. Go to **Message Board**
2. Click **New Post**
3. Type your message
4. **Attach media** (optional): Click 📎 to add photos/videos
5. Click **Post**

#### Replying to Messages

1. Click on any message to view the thread
2. Type your reply in the reply box
3. Click **Reply**

#### Pinning Important Messages

1. Click the ⋮ menu on a message
2. Select **Pin Message** (Admin only)
3. Pinned messages appear at the top of the board

#### Reacting with Emoji

1. Hover over a message
2. Click the 😊 emoji button
3. Select an emoji to react

### Private Chat

For one-on-one conversations:

1. Go to **Chat**
2. Click **New Chat**
3. Select a family member
4. Start typing your message
5. Click **Send**

**Features**:
- Read receipts (see when messages are read)
- Typing indicators (see when someone is typing)
- Online/offline status

### Real-time Updates

All messages update in real-time across all devices. You'll see:
- New messages instantly
- Typing indicators
- Online status of family members
- Read receipts

## Calendar & Events

### Creating an Event

1. Go to **Calendar**
2. Click **New Event** or click on a date
3. Fill in event details:
   - **Event name**
   - **Date & time**
   - **Location** (optional)
   - **Description**
   - **Visibility**: Who can see this event
4. Click **Create Event**

### RSVP to Events

1. Go to **Calendar**
2. Click on an event
3. Click **RSVP** and select:
   - ✅ **Going**
   - ❓ **Maybe**
   - ❌ **Not Going**
4. Add a comment (optional)

### Event Visibility

Control who can see your events:
- **Family Only**: All family members
- **Specific Members**: Choose who can see
- **Private**: Only you

## Shopping Lists

### Creating a Shopping List

1. Go to **Shopping Lists**
2. Click **New List**
3. Enter a **list name** (e.g., "Grocery Shopping")
4. Set a **due date** (optional)
5. Click **Create**

### Adding Items

1. Open a shopping list
2. Click **Add Item**
3. Enter the **item name**
4. Select a **category** (Produce, Dairy, Meat, etc.)
5. Click **Add**

### Managing Items

- **Check off items**: Click the checkbox when purchased
- **Edit items**: Click the item to edit name/category
- **Delete items**: Click the ⋮ menu → **Delete**
- **Assign items**: Click an item → **Assign to** → Select member

### Sharing Lists

1. Open a shopping list
2. Click **Share**
3. Select family members to share with
4. Choose permission level:
   - **View Only**: Can see but not edit
   - **Edit**: Can add/edit/delete items
5. Click **Share**

## Photo Gallery

### Uploading Photos

1. Go to **Gallery**
2. Click **Upload Photos**
3. Select photos from your device
4. Add **captions** (optional)
5. Click **Upload**

### Organizing Photos

1. Go to **Gallery**
2. Click **Create Album**
3. Enter an **album name**
4. Click **Create**
5. Drag photos into the album

### Viewing Photos

1. Go to **Gallery**
2. Click on a photo to view full-size
3. Use arrow keys or buttons to navigate
4. Click the ⋮ menu for options:
   - **Download**: Save to your device
   - **Share**: Share with external link
   - **Delete**: Remove from gallery

### AI Photo Tagging

Photos are automatically tagged with AI:
- **People**: Detected faces
- **Objects**: Items in the photo
- **Scenes**: Location/setting
- **Activities**: What's happening

You can edit tags manually to improve organization.

## Video Calls

### Starting a Video Call

1. Go to **Video Calls**
2. Click **Start Call**
3. Select family members to invite
4. Click **Start**

Family members will receive a notification to join.

### Joining a Call

1. Click the notification or go to **Video Calls**
2. Click **Join Call**
3. Allow camera/microphone access
4. Click **Join**

### During a Call

- **Mute/Unmute**: Click 🎤 button
- **Start/Stop Video**: Click 📹 button
- **Share Screen**: Click 🖥️ button (if supported)
- **End Call**: Click ❌ button

### Call Quality

For best video quality:
- Use a stable internet connection (5+ Mbps)
- Close other apps using bandwidth
- Ensure good lighting
- Use headphones to reduce echo

## Streaming Theater

### Accessing Streaming Services

1. Go to **Theater**
2. Browse available streaming sources:
   - **Pluto TV**: Free live TV
   - **Tubi**: Free movies & shows
   - **Roku**: Roku Channel content
   - **Freeview**: Free streaming

### Watching Content

1. Click on a streaming source
2. Browse or search for content
3. Click to play
4. Use playback controls:
   - ▶️ Play/Pause
   - ⏩ Fast forward
   - ⏪ Rewind
   - 🔊 Volume

### Parental Controls

Parents can restrict content:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Parental Controls**
2. Set **age rating limits**:
   - G: General Audiences
   - PG: Parental Guidance
   - PG-13: Parents Strongly Cautioned
   - R: Restricted
3. Click **Save**

Children's accounts will only see content matching the rating.

## Settings & Preferences

### Profile Settings

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Edit your:
   - **Display name**
   - **Profile photo**
   - **Bio**
   - **Email address**
3. Click **Save**

### Language & Localization

1. Go to **Settings** → **Language**
2. Select your preferred language
3. The interface updates immediately

Supported languages:
- English
- Spanish
- French
- German
- Italian
- Portuguese
- Chinese (Simplified)
- Japanese

### Notification Settings

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Choose notification preferences:
   - **In-App Alerts**: Notifications within the app
   - **Email Notifications**: Email alerts
   - **Daily Digest**: Summary email
   - **Weekly Digest**: Weekly summary

3. Select which events trigger notifications:
   - New messages
   - Event invitations
   - Calendar reminders
   - Photo uploads
   - Member activity

### Privacy & Security

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy & Security**
2. Configure:
   - **Profile visibility**: Who can see your profile
   - **Activity status**: Show when you're online
   - **Read receipts**: Show when you've read messages
   - **Two-factor authentication**: Enable 2FA

### Data & Storage

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data & Storage**
2. View:
   - **Storage used**: How much space you're using
   - **Storage limit**: Your plan's limit
   - **Backup status**: When your data was last backed up
3. Click **Request Data Export** to download your data

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is FamilyHub free?**  
A: Yes! FamilyHub has a free plan with basic features. Pro and Enterprise plans offer additional features.

**Q: How many family members can I invite?**  
A: Free plan: 5 members. Pro plan: 20 members. Enterprise: Unlimited.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes! We use end-to-end encryption for messages and store data on secure servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I use FamilyHub on mobile?**  
A: Yes! FamilyHub works on any device with a web browser. Native mobile apps coming soon.

### Account & Billing

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Yes! Go to Settings → Profile → Email Address.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support@familyhub.app for refunds.

### Features

**Q: Can I delete messages?**  
A: Yes! Click the ⋮ menu on a message and select "Delete". Deleted messages are removed for everyone.

**Q: How do I share photos outside the family?**  
A: Go to Gallery → Click photo → Share → Generate link. Share the link with anyone.

**Q: Can I schedule events in advance?**  
A: Yes! Create an event with a future date. Family members will be notified.

**Q: How do video calls work?**  
A: FamilyHub uses Jitsi Meet for video calls. No additional software needed — calls work directly in your browser.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: Messages aren't updating in real-time.**  
A: Check your internet connection. Refresh the page. If the issue persists, contact support.

**Q: I can't upload photos.**  
A: Check your storage limit (Settings → Data & Storage). Upgrade your plan if needed.

**Q: Video calls are laggy.**  
A: Close other apps using bandwidth. Use a wired internet connection if possible.

**Q: I forgot my family's surname.**  
A: Go to Family Settings → Family Info. Your surname is displayed there.
