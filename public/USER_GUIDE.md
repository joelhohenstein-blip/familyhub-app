## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit FamilyHub** at https://familyhub-d562.pre.dev
2. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. **Enter your email address** and create a secure password
4. **Verify your email** — check your inbox for a confirmation link
5. **Complete your profile:**
   - First name
   - Last name
   - Profile photo (optional)
   - Timezone (for accurate timestamps)

### Creating Your First Family

After signing up, you'll be prompted to create your first family:

1. **Enter your family surname** (e.g., "Smith", "Johnson")
   - This is the primary identifier for your family group
   - Only the family admin can change this
2. **Invite family members** by email (optional — you can add them later)
3. **Review and confirm** — your family is created!

**Note:** You are automatically set as the **Family Admin** for any family you create. Admins have special permissions for moderation, member management, and settings.

### Joining an Existing Family

If someone has invited you to their family:

1. **Check your email** for an invitation from FamilyHub
2. **Click the invitation link** or copy the family code
3. **Accept the invitation** — you'll be added to the family immediately
4. **Complete your profile** if you haven't already

## Core Features

### 💬 Messaging & Conversations

FamilyHub's messaging system keeps your family connected with threaded conversations and real-time updates.

#### Sending Messages

1. **Navigate to the Message Board** from the main menu
2. **Click "New Message"** or select an existing thread
3. **Type your message** in the text box
4. **Add attachments** (photos, videos, files) by clicking the attachment icon
5. **Press Enter** or click **Send** to post

#### Threaded Conversations

- **Reply to a message** by clicking the reply icon
- **View the full thread** by clicking on any message
- **Nested replies** show the conversation flow clearly
- **Collapse/expand threads** to manage your view

#### Message Features

- **Emojis & Reactions** — Click the emoji icon to add reactions to messages
- **Pin Important Messages** — Admins can pin messages to keep them visible
- **Search Messages** — Use the search bar to find past conversations
- **Edit Messages** — Click the three-dot menu to edit your own messages (within 5 minutes)
- **Delete Messages** — Remove messages you've sent (admins can delete any message)

#### Online Presence & Typing Indicators

- **Green dot** next to a member's name = currently online
- **"User is typing..."** appears when someone is composing a message
- **Last seen** timestamp shows when a member was last active

### 🎥 Video Calls

Connect face-to-face with family members using built-in video calling.

#### Starting a Video Call

1. **Navigate to a family member's profile**
2. **Click the "Video Call" button**
3. **Wait for them to accept** — they'll see a notification
4. **Once connected**, you can:
   - Mute/unmute your microphone
   - Turn your camera on/off
   - Share your screen (desktop only)
   - End the call

#### During a Call

- **Mute/Unmute** — Click the microphone icon
- **Camera On/Off** — Click the camera icon
- **Screen Share** — Click the screen icon (desktop only)
- **Chat** — Send text messages during the call
- **End Call** — Click the red hang-up button

**Note:** Video calls are one-on-one. For group calls, use the **Streaming Theater** feature to watch content together.

### 🖼️ Media Gallery

Store, organize, and share family photos and videos in one central location.

#### Uploading Photos & Videos

1. **Navigate to the Media Gallery**
2. **Click "Upload"** or drag-and-drop files
3. **Add a caption** (optional)
4. **Tag family members** (optional) — they'll be notified
5. **Set privacy level:**
   - **Public** — all family members can see
   - **Private** — only you can see
   - **Shared with...** — select specific members

#### Organizing Your Gallery

- **View by date** — photos are grouped by upload date
- **Search by caption** — find photos by keywords
- **Filter by member** — see photos of specific people
- **Create albums** — group related photos together

#### Smart Features

- **AI Photo Tagging** — Photos are automatically tagged with people, places, and objects
- **Photo Timeline** — View your family's visual history in chronological order
- **Favorites** — Star your favorite photos for quick access

#### Sharing & Downloading

- **Download** — Click the download icon to save to your device
- **Share** — Generate a shareable link (expires after 30 days)
- **Print** — Order physical prints (Premium feature)

### 📆 Shared Calendar

Keep your family organized with a shared calendar for events, birthdays, and important dates.

#### Creating an Event

1. **Navigate to the Calendar**
2. **Click on a date** or click "New Event"
3. **Enter event details:**
   - Event name
   - Date & time
   - Location (optional)
   - Description
   - Attendees (select family members)
4. **Set reminders** — get notified before the event
5. **Save the event**

#### Event Types

- **Birthday** — automatically recurring yearly
- **Anniversary** — automatically recurring yearly
- **Appointment** — one-time or recurring
- **Family Activity** — group events
- **Holiday** — pre-populated holidays for your region

#### Calendar Views

- **Month view** — see all events at a glance
- **Week view** — detailed view of upcoming events
- **Day view** — focus on today's schedule
- **Agenda view** — list of upcoming events

#### Notifications

- **Get reminders** 1 day, 1 hour, or 15 minutes before an event
- **See who's attending** — check RSVP status
- **Update your RSVP** — confirm or decline attendance

### 🛒 Shopping Lists

Coordinate grocery shopping and household tasks with shared shopping lists.

#### Creating a Shopping List

1. **Navigate to Shopping Lists**
2. **Click "New List"**
3. **Name your list** (e.g., "Weekly Groceries", "Party Supplies")
4. **Set a due date** (optional)
5. **Invite members** to collaborate

#### Adding Items

1. **Click "Add Item"**
2. **Enter the item name** (e.g., "Milk", "Bread")
3. **Select a category:**
   - Produce
   - Dairy
   - Meat & Seafood
   - Pantry
   - Frozen
   - Household
   - Other
4. **Assign to a member** (optional) — they'll be notified
5. **Set quantity** (optional)

#### Managing Your List

- **Check off items** as you shop
- **Edit items** — click to modify name, quantity, or category
- **Delete items** — remove completed or unwanted items
- **Sort by category** — organize your shopping trip
- **Share with others** — send a link to family members

#### Smart Features

- **Price tracking** — see estimated costs (Premium)
- **Recipe integration** — add ingredients from recipes
- **Store locator** — find items at nearby stores (Premium)

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

### Common Issues

#### I can't log in

**Solution:**
1. Check that you're using the correct email address
2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset your password
3. Check your spam folder for password reset emails
4. Clear your browser cache and try again
5. Try a different browser or device

#### Video calls aren't working

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure your camera and microphone are enabled
3. Check browser permissions — allow camera/microphone access
4. Disable browser extensions (they can interfere)
5. Try a different browser
6. Restart your device

#### Photos aren't uploading

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure the file is under 100MB
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, MP4, MOV
4. Try uploading a smaller file first
5. Clear your browser cache

#### I'm not receiving notifications

**Solution:**
1. Check notification settings in Settings > Notifications
2. Ensure notifications are enabled for your browser
3. Check your device's notification settings
4. Disable browser notification blockers
5. Try logging out and back in

#### Messages aren't syncing

**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Restart your device

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many family members can I invite?**
A: Unlimited! You can invite as many family members as you'd like.

**Q: Can I be part of multiple families?**
A: Yes! You can join multiple families and switch between them easily.

**Q: How long are messages stored?**
A: Messages are stored indefinitely. You can search and access past conversations anytime.

**Q: Can I delete messages?**
A: Yes, you can delete your own messages. Family admins can delete any message.

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We take privacy seriously.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can request a data export from Settings > Account. We'll email you a ZIP file with all your data.

**Q: What happens if I delete my account?**
A: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted after 30 days. During this period, you can cancel the deletion.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Click the three-dot menu on any message or photo and select "Report". Our moderation team will review it.

**Q: Can I change my family surname?**
A: Only family admins can change the family surname. Contact your admin if you need to update it.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: FamilyHub is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. Native apps are coming soon!
