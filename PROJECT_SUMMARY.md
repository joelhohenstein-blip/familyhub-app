## 🎯 Project Overview

**FamilyHub** is a comprehensive family communication and coordination platform that brings families closer together through secure messaging, video calls, calendar coordination, photo sharing, and memory preservation.

### Mission
To provide families with a private, ad-free, secure platform for communication, coordination, and memory sharing—all without requiring a credit card or subscription.

### Vision
Become the #1 trusted family communication platform globally, used by millions of families to stay connected and organized.
