## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. **Visit FamilyHub**
   - Go to https://familyhub.app
   - Click "Sign Up" button

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password (8+ characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
   - Enter your full name
   - Click "Create Account"

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - Your account is now active!

### Sign In

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Visit https://familyhub.app/login
   - Or click "Sign In" on the home page

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Click "Sign In"

3. **You're In!**
   - You'll be taken to your dashboard
   - If you haven't created a family yet, you'll see the setup wizard

### Forgot Password?

1. **Click "Forgot Password"** on the login page
2. **Enter your email address**
3. **Check your email** for password reset link
4. **Create a new password**
5. **Sign in** with your new password

## Creating Your Family

### Step 1: Start the Setup Wizard

When you first sign in, you'll see the "Create Your Family" wizard.

1. **Click "Create Family"**
2. **Enter family name** (e.g., "The Smiths", "Johnson Family")
3. **Choose family type:**
   - Nuclear Family (parents + children)
   - Extended Family (grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins)
   - Blended Family (step-parents, step-siblings)
   - Other

### Step 2: Add Family Members

1. **Click "Add Member"**
2. **Enter member details:**
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Relationship to you (parent, sibling, child, grandparent, etc.)
   - Date of birth (optional)
   - Phone number (optional)

3. **Send Invitation**
   - Click "Send Invite"
   - They'll receive an email invitation
   - Once they accept, they'll be added to your family

### Step 3: Customize Your Family

1. **Upload Family Photo** (optional)
   - Click family avatar
   - Choose image from your device
   - Crop and adjust as needed

2. **Set Family Preferences**
   - Time zone
   - Language
   - Privacy settings

3. **You're Ready!**
   - Your family is now set up
   - Start inviting more members

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In

**Problem:** Forgot password
- Click "Forgot Password"
- Enter email
- Check email for reset link
- Create new password

**Problem:** Account locked
- Wait 15 minutes
- Try again
- Contact support if still locked

**Problem:** Email not verified
- Check spam folder
- Click "Resend Verification Email"
- Click link in email

### Messages Not Sending

**Problem:** Message stuck
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page
- Try again

**Problem:** Can't see messages
- Refresh page
- Check if conversation is muted
- Check notification settings

### Calendar Issues

**Problem:** Events not showing
- Refresh page
- Check calendar view
- Check if events are hidden

**Problem:** Can't create event
- Check date is in future
- Check all required fields filled
- Try different browser

### Photo Upload Issues

**Problem:** Photos won't upload
- Check file size (max 50MB)
- Check file format (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP)
- Check internet connection
- Try uploading fewer photos

**Problem:** Photos look blurry
- Check original image quality
- Try uploading higher resolution
- Check zoom level in browser

### Video Call Issues

**Problem:** Can't start call
- Check camera/microphone permissions
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Restart browser

**Problem:** Audio/video not working
- Check device permissions
- Check microphone/camera in settings
- Try different device
- Restart application

**Problem:** Call keeps dropping
- Check internet connection
- Move closer to WiFi router
- Close other applications
- Try wired connection

### General Issues

**Problem:** App is slow
- Clear browser cache
- Close other tabs
- Check internet speed
- Try different browser

**Problem:** Features not working
- Refresh page
- Clear cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Contact support

**Problem:** Getting error messages
- Note the error code
- Take screenshot
- Contact support with details

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is FamilyHub free?**
A: Yes! FamilyHub has a free plan with basic features. Premium plans available for advanced features.

**Q: How many family members can I add?**
A: Free plan: up to 5 members. Plus/Premium: unlimited members.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! We use bank-level encryption. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

**Q: Can I use FamilyHub on mobile?**
A: Yes! FamilyHub works on all devices. Download the mobile app or use the web version.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently deleted.

### Family Management

**Q: Can I have multiple families?**
A: Currently, one family per account. Contact support if you need multiple families.

**Q: What if a family member doesn't have email?**
A: You can add them manually and manage their account. They can sign in with a phone number instead.

**Q: Can I change the family name?**
A: Yes! Go to Family Settings > Edit Family Name.

**Q: What happens if I remove a family member?**
A: They lose access to family content. They can rejoin if invited again.

### Messaging

**Q: Can I delete messages?**
A: Yes! Click message > Delete. Deleted messages are removed for everyone.

**Q: Can I edit messages?**
A: Yes! Click message > Edit. Changes are visible to everyone.

**Q: Can I search old messages?**
A: Yes! Use the search box in Messages. Search by keywords, sender, or date.

**Q: Are messages encrypted?**
A: Yes! All messages are encrypted end-to-end.

### Calendar & Events

**Q: Can I share calendar with non-family members?**
A: No, calendar is family-only. But you can share individual events via link.

**Q: Can I set recurring events?**
A: Yes! When creating event, choose repeat frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly).

**Q: Can I get reminders for events?**
A: Yes! Set reminders when creating event (15 min, 1 hour, 1 day before).

**Q: Can I see everyone's calendar?**
A: Yes! You can view all family members' events in the shared calendar.

### Photos & Gallery

**Q: How much storage do I get?**
A: Free: 5GB. Plus: 100GB. Premium: Unlimited.

**Q: What file formats are supported?**
A: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, HEIC, BMP.

**Q: Can I download my photos?**
A: Yes! Click photo > Download. Or download entire album.

**Q: Are my photos backed up?**
A: Yes! All photos are automatically backed up to our secure servers.

### Video Calls

**Q: How many people can be on a call?**
A: Up to 50 people on a single call.

**Q: Can I record calls?**
A: Yes! Click "Record" during call. Recording saved to gallery.

**Q: Do I need special software?**
A: No! Video calls work in your browser. No downloads needed.

**Q: Can I share my screen?**
A: Yes! Click "Share Screen" during call.

### Billing

**Q: Can I change my plan anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at next billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes! 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support for refund.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.

**Q: Is there a family discount?**
A: Yes! Family plans available. Contact support for pricing.

**Q: Can I get an invoice?**
A: Yes! Go to Billing > Billing History > Download Invoice.

### Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Click "Help" > "Contact Support" or email support@familyhub.app.

**Q: What are support hours?**
A: 24/7 support available. Response time: 1-2 hours.

**Q: Is there a knowledge base?**
A: Yes! Visit help.familyhub.app for articles and tutorials.

**Q: Can I request a feature?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings > Feedback > Request Feature.
