## 📞 How to Exercise Your Rights

### Request Access to Your Data
1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Rights
2. Click "Request My Data"
3. We'll email you a copy within 30 days

### Request Data Deletion
1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Rights
2. Click "Delete My Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Your data will be deleted within 30 days

### Manage Cookie Preferences
1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Cookies
2. Toggle cookie categories on/off
3. Changes apply immediately

### Contact Privacy Team
- **Email:** privacy@familyhub.app
- **Mail:** [Your Address]
- **In-App:** Settings → Help → Contact Support

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Do you use cookies?
**Yes.** We use cookies for authentication, language preference, and analytics. See [Cookie Policy](./COOKIE_POLICY.md) for details.

### Do you sell my data?
**No.** We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties. See [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

### Can I delete my account?
**Yes.** You can delete your account anytime in Settings → Privacy → Data Rights. Your data will be deleted within 30 days.

### How do I contact you about privacy?
**Email:** privacy@familyhub.app  
**In-App:** Settings → Help → Contact Support

### What if I'm in the EU?
You have additional rights under GDPR. See [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) section 6.1 for details.

### What if I'm in California?
You have additional rights under CCPA. See [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) section 6.2 for details.

### How long do you keep my data?
We keep your data as long as you use FamilyHub. After deletion, we keep backups for 30 days, then permanently delete. See [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) section 5.2 for details.
